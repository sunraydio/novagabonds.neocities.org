KINGDOM HEARTS

BIG BIG CREDIT to [Televo] for having HUNDREDS of assets for me to pick through... I yam grateful forever

Kingdom Hearts the greatest game series of all time... I'm currently trying to acquire as many of the games as possible but as of current I only have 358/2 Days. which to be fair is the only one that really matters to me lmao.

no, but, a little background for you; I played the games when I was really little and while I don't remember half of it (and am pretty sure most of the lore I know was provided to me by my brother!) it shaped me fundamentally, especially the one game that I DO have. the grief of memory? the grief of its absence? who even knows how to properly describe how absolutely heartwrenching it was first playthrough. it felt real to me. it was one of the first stories I had the opportunity to truly escape into as a kid. I still think about it when I'm staring up at the ceiling and thinking about what it means to make something good.

all in all 10/10 game I recommend it forever. or even just to watch all the cutscenes on youtube (as the broke kids of the 2010s used to do)
