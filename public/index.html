Welcome!

Welcome to the resting place for all things Novagabonds! Here you’ll find all my works-in-progress of the past few years, from sprawling comic universes to simple coding projects. You can get updates on this site via following my neocities, & find me posting art on tumblr!

This site uses Javascript & works best on a Firefox desktop browser.

About Me

Hey! I'm Ink, a Southeast Asian artist making human art and telling human stories. I started making things when I was little and never really stopped, and the whole thing--my love of it, the scope--only really continues to grow, which you're looking at an example of in this site.

I'm big on art, and music, and writing, and movies, annnndd a whole lot of other things you'll find in my actual about page. hope you enjoy your stay!
