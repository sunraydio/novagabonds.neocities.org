Fruiperheroes

Fruiperheroes is a comic project that mpekamitzii and i have been working on since 2017. It follows Shane, Kit, and Mira as they discover just how important it is to trust each other as they uncover the ominous undercurrent that lies below Isla Angula.

Main Trio

Mira, Kit and Shane are the main characters of the series (although the side characters are just as beloved to us)! there was once three of us on the team, so each of us contributed one character to the main cast, although we have had shared custody of Kit for quite a while now.

Which one's mine you ask?...

Originally, I'd had an entirely different main character. Delaney Jodhveer, the local strongwoman who always bit off just a little more than she could chew, still has a special place in my heart--she's me, or how I was when I was much younger and much more scared. She was the character I wanted to make to prove that fat people could be more than the side friend, more than the butt of every joke; she was the character I wanted to make to prove that I could be more than those things.

Yet, something still nagged at me--I was the only person of color on the team, and yet all of our characters were white. As much as I do still adore Del, and as important as she still is to moving the story forward, the three of us agreed something needed to change--and thus, Mira, one of Delaney's best friends in their whole world, began to take form as part of the main trio, and Delaney took a big step back.

Now, about four years since the transition (ha), Mira's grown into a character that is incredibly dear to me--a mess of contradiction, flaky yet reliable at the same time, immovably chill yet irreprehensibly angry. They're the embodiment of what I know about people now: who they can be, who they have been, who they are.

And, once we finally start pushing into the real production of this comic, I hope that you'll come to love all these moving parts of them, too.
